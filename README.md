# CycleScan
CSCI 1470 Final Project Cycle Scan using CycleGAN to transform between knee MRIs of CISS and DESS Images

Folder Stucture
- 2yrCISS_Dicoms: contains 96 folders of each subject that had a CISS scan at their 2 year post-operation visit. Scans are of the non-surgical limb 
- 6yrDESS_Dicoms: contains 68 folders of that contains the subject's DESS scan take at their 6 year post-operation visit. Scans are of the non-surgical limb
